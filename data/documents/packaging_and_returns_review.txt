Packaging and Returns Review - Mid-Year Assessment

Packaging Quality Issues:
23% of customers reported receiving items with damaged packaging.
Fragile items in the home goods category had the highest damage rate at 34%.
The current packaging standard was last updated 18 months ago and does not account for
the increase in long-distance shipments introduced this year.

Root Causes Identified:
1. Single-layer boxing used for items that require double-walled corrugated packaging.
2. Insufficient void fill material for irregularly shaped products.
3. No fragile labelling applied consistently across the warehouse.

Returns Data:
Total returns in H1: 1,240 units
Returns due to damage in transit: 610 units (49% of all returns)
Returns due to wrong item received: 290 units (23% of all returns)
Returns due to product not as described: 340 units (27% of all returns)

Financial Impact:
Estimated cost of returns processing: SGD 18,600 in H1
Estimated cost of replacement shipments: SGD 11,200 in H1
Total estimated loss from packaging-related issues: SGD 29,800 in H1

Recommendations:
1. Upgrade packaging standards for fragile and long-distance shipments immediately.
2. Introduce a packaging audit process at the warehouse level.
3. Apply fragile stickers and handling instructions to all relevant SKUs.
4. Review the returns portal UX to reduce customer friction and processing time.
