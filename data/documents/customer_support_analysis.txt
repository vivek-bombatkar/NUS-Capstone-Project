Customer Support Analysis Report - Q1 and Q2

Executive Summary:
Support ticket volume increased by 31% over the first half of the year.
The majority of tickets were related to delivery status inquiries and return requests.

Response Time Metrics:
Average first response time: 18 hours (target: 4 hours)
Average resolution time: 52 hours (target: 24 hours)
Ticket backlog at end of Q2: 340 unresolved tickets

Top Ticket Categories:
1. Delivery status inquiry - 42% of all tickets
2. Return and refund requests - 28% of all tickets
3. Product defect complaints - 18% of all tickets
4. Billing and payment issues - 8% of all tickets
5. General inquiries - 4% of all tickets

Agent Performance:
The support team is currently understaffed by an estimated 3 full-time agents.
Peak ticket volume occurs between 6pm and 10pm on weekdays.
Customer satisfaction scores for resolved tickets average 3.1 out of 5.

Recommendations:
1. Hire 2-3 additional support agents for evening shifts.
2. Introduce an automated chatbot to handle delivery status inquiries.
3. Create a self-service returns portal to reduce ticket volume by an estimated 25%.
4. Set up escalation rules to prioritise tickets older than 24 hours.
